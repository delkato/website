Sturdy and durable.

About Us

A-1 Modern Upholstery is a family-owned and operated local Jacksonville business. The owner, Carlos Delgado, personally works on and oversees every upholstery project to make sure every job is nothing less than perfect. We always worry about the details and seek to ensure that every job is of the highest quality. We've even reupholstered furniture featured in movies, like Sunshine State.
